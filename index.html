What is Decentralised_

Imagine a new dimension to the internet open to everyone, in rich highly detailed 3D, owned only by its users.

Assets created by users of Decentralised are stored in a peer to peer blockchain database shared securely between everyone connected to the network.

With the upcoming release of various virtual reality devices such as Microsoft HoloLens and Oculus Rift the future of networked 3D is an exciting space. The aim of the Decentralised project is to develop a commercially viable, free, open, secure and wild environment for individuals, business, communication and cutting edge experience to meet.

The Decentralised blockchain is based on Bitcoin and works in a similar way. The 3D environment is a continuous ever expanding landscape created by 'Miners' who are rewarded for processing the blockchain with ownership of virtual space. Virtual space can be bought and sold, and other transactions made using Bitcoin inside the Decentralised viewer.

The Decentralised viewer user interface is loosly based on Firestorm Viewer as this is a mature, popular and familiar way to navigate a virtual environment, although due to the fundamentally different platform architecture the features vary.
